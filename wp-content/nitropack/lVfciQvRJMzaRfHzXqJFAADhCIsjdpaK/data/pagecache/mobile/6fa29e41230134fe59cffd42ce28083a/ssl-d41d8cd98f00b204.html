Frequently Asked Questions

You might have questions, we have answers.

What is Restires.com?

Restires is the best mobile tire shop in Orlando, we provide professional tire service for your vehicle at the confort of your doorstep or parking lot.

What are the services provided by Restires.com?

We provide new tires, tire installation, tire balancing, tire repairs, tire rotation, TPMS service and Fleet services.

How long do the tires take to deliver?

In emergency cases, tires can be sourced as early as same day. But most non-emergency cases can be sourced and installed within 24-48 hours

How can I be sure I’m ordering the correct tire size?

By shopping on Restires.com and entering your vehicle information, you will find the accurate tire sizing information for your exact vehicle. We also recommend doing a quick physical check of the tire size markings on your current tires to ensure a 100% exact fit.

At what time Restires.com opens?

We open from 8am to 8pm from Monday through Sunday.

Do you attend roadside breakdowns?

Yes, although most of our work is done by appointment only. In case of availability, we can attend roadside breakdowns. Anyway, we will not be able to help you when you are on a highway, only when you are in a safe place, like a parking lot. For us the safety of our employees is a priority

Do you sell used tires?

No, due to safety concerns with unknown used tire history, we have a strict “NO USED TIRE” policy

Do the tires come with any warranty?

All tires sold come with their default tire manufacturers warranty against manufacturing defects. Some manufacturers also provide a built in Road Hazard Warranty. Please see individual manufacturer information for specific details and also we offer road hazard warranty with the purchase of any tire, as well as many other perk  including discounts, free shipping, flat battery jumps, and more
