THE BEST MOBILE TIRE INSTALLATION

When it comes to mounting and balancing your tires, you must rely on true professionals, such as those at Restires.com™. Ensuring your’s, your family, and your friend’s safety is our top priority.

Convenient

Our mobile tire installation service is specially designed to cause the least amount of disruption to your daily life. Full tire service

Tire balancing is a fundamental part of a correct tire installation; therefore, our mobile tire installation service always includes the tire balancing service.

Fast and easy service request

As soon as we receive your service request, we will contact you to arrange a time and place to service your vehicle.

Restires.com™ offers mobile services for mounting and balancing your tires. This task is essential to drive with maximum safety and comfort, as it avoids steering wheel vibrations and premature wear of your wheels.

The purpose of balancing, which must be performed after every tire change or after a repair, is to distribute the weight between them very accurately. It is not a trivial matter; therefore, it must be carried out by experts capable of successfully completing the whole process since we must not forget that even minor changes to the tire can cause an imbalance.

This task requires specialized knowledge and specific technical equipment to position, assemble, and balance the entire wheel. The imbalance is compensated by the specific attachment of small counterweights on the rim edge, correcting the problem and eliminating the vibration.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long it takes to install my new set of tires?

Installing and balancing a tire takes an average of 30 minutes; therefore, to install four tires, the service can take approximately 2 hours.

Is there any difference between Mobile tire Installation and Conventional Tire installation?

No, there is no difference. We use the same equipment that a conventional tire place uses, the only difference is that we take the tire shop to your location.

Can I get my tires installed during the weekend?

Yes, at Restires.com, we work from Monday to Friday from 8:00 am
to 8:00 pm, Saturdays from 8:00 am to 3:00 pm, and Sundays from
9:00 am to 1:00 pm.

Does tire installation include tire balancing?

Yes, tire balancing is a fundamental part of proper tire installation; therefore, our installation service always includes tire balancing service.
